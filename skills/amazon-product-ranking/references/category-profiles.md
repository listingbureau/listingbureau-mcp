## Seasonal Categories

Products in these categories have significant demand variation throughout the year. Campaign timing matters.

| Category | Peak Period | Ramp Start (Ideal) |
|----------|:----------:|:-------------------:|
| Toys & Games | Nov-Dec (holiday) | Sept-Oct |
| Garden & Outdoor | Mar-Jun (spring/summer) | Jan-Feb |
| Sports & Outdoors (summer) | May-Aug | Mar-Apr |
| Sports & Outdoors (winter) | Nov-Feb | Sep-Oct |
| Back to School | Jul-Aug | May-Jun |
| Grills & Outdoor Cooking | Apr-Jul | Feb-Mar |
| Pool & Water | May-Aug | Mar-Apr |
| Holiday Decorations | Oct-Dec | Aug-Sep |

**Recommendation:** Start ranking campaigns 4-8 weeks before peak demand. This allows time to reach page 1 before the high-volume season drives organic sales.

## Using This Reference

1. **Identify the product's Amazon category** from the product page or user input
2. **Look up the default profile** in the category table above
3. **Adjust based on specifics:**
   - Price significantly above category average? Consider moving one tier toward High-Price/Considered
   - Price significantly below category average? Consider moving one tier toward Low-Price/Consumable
   - New launch during honeymoon? Override to Aggressive regardless of category
   - Re-ranking campaign? Override to Conservative regardless of category
4. **Use the referral fee** for SFB proceeds calculation
5. **Check seasonality** and adjust campaign timing recommendation if applicable
